Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [Current Date]

Important: TalkToBuddha is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data and do not track or identify individual users.

1. Information We Collect

TalkToBuddha collects very limited information:

  • Chat Conversations: Your chat messages are stored locally on your device using Realm database for the app to function properly
  • App Preferences: Your meditation sound preferences and onboarding status are stored locally
  • Usage Count: We track the number of chat interactions locally to enforce freemium limits
  • Purchase Information: Apple handles all in-app purchase data through StoreKit

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide AI-powered Buddhist guidance through OpenAI's API
  • Maintain your chat history locally on your device
  • Remember your app preferences and settings
  • Enforce freemium usage limits
  • Process in-app purchases through Apple's systems

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share minimal information with third parties:

  • OpenAI: Your chat messages are sent to OpenAI's API to generate responses. OpenAI's privacy policy applies to this data
  • Apple: Purchase information is handled by Apple through StoreKit
  • No Analytics: We do not use analytics services, crash reporting, or advertising networks
  • No Social Media: We do not integrate with social media platforms

4. Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device using Realm database. We do not:

  • Store your data on external servers
  • Collect personal identification information
  • Track your location or device identifiers
  • Use cookies or web tracking technologies

5. Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete all chat conversations using the app's delete function
  • Reset app preferences through device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Contact us to request data deletion assistance

6. Children's Privacy

TalkToBuddha is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and our app does not require user registration or personal data collection.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or our website.
